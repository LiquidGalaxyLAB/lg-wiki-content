---
title: HOW TO STOP THE EARTH FROM SPINNING CONTINUOUSLY IN THE MASTER VM
contributor: Baikuntha B Bihari
date: 2024-06-11T13:34:56.668+00:00
---

  
**After Launching the LG in the VMs, the earth spins continuously, and to stop that, we need to follow some simple steps, which are as follows:**  
  
**1 - After LG has been successfully launched, you will see the menu bar in the top left corner, then go to “Tools” >> “Options”**  
  
![entry_3_image_1](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXdCwFdehaYppvWxOQUrafaii9R28b1f3OjWRehk2Jk6RS58MTEEx5ZeX-Sv-P4KL0jLiG8Lb3dMAdDoeM4CQpqYfkwo4BaY-4X8rpSuzKS4qbVA1NTn5pW1wxPWmqkMb3oa0khErFLcNwZ2NhUKAWddoREM?key=7OhUbKXFfPMCeviapUEvVQ "entry_3_image_1")  
**2 - Then click on “Navigation” >> “Restore Default” >> “Apply”**  
![entry_3_image_2](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXewfB16SMHOI35xn3WFxmNvBVmxivjHbpkmi78lVCl-FRfcpj_UlKRCkQmzZxA_8pWanvja6hj7LDUXYTUgjbgM3QFSoIjsuk2A7M0cEKBsbmhf6n1gYNb2M8pG5vztNJI031TEtuN8LEmtWQniAhTEqFBS?key=7OhUbKXFfPMCeviapUEvVQ "entry_3_image_2")  
![entry_3_image_3](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXfYMcxBEsF_kSI71DNQoHgKxe8P574Bbe8Tn3OgJbiEU4IF8V8nVHLOuc1umeatZwLUSvNjiVYu1IQyHfv54R10PjW0b7V9Dg6QltGxtKMAirSIXXkITLJGkLVFKc4mfcVM6jKtxOXnky6aVpyPaXQjlgif?key=7OhUbKXFfPMCeviapUEvVQ "entry_3_image_3")  
**3 - Then, two pop-ups will come up. You just have to click “ok” for each one.**  
  
**4 - Then, at last, just click “OK” on the bottom, and it will stop spinning, and you are all set to go.**
