## Overview
To Stop the earth from spinning on startup  
Go to tools > Navigation  
And now set the Fly-To-Speed to minimum and click apply.  
  
![earth](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXfOTzZXZ6B-dCoS_EeaqJscaFUm0vzEBk9shfpYSNNo9wNkQaHc00uC2oAQmhtXsmSROWQ7oIbkaQNhLODZ0mwEEH7uTe_EglPD-1mSdW6Y8XgcfBO52flyvHKTzWEh2OvAIWqE_-DQTG5yRu7qKJ8klmtQdhatFicxCryP5f03xNbT_LPAd-M?key=KSe51dGJJaMYpZ4SWElSIg "earth") Then after clicking apply you may see couple of pop-up just click OK on both.  
  
Now after applying changes, don’t exit the menu and click Restore Defaults.  
Now click apply and then you can exit by clicking OK.  
The Earth will now stop spinning.
